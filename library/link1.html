About the Library

The Kerala University Library is a hub of knowledge and resources, serving the academic community of Kerala University. With a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources, it plays a pivotal role in supporting research and learning activities.

Opening Hours

The library is open on weekdays from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. It is closed on Sundays and public holidays.
